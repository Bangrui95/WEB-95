Audio-reactive Live Visual Performance

TASTE is a live audio-visual performance shaped by music, motion, and collective energy. In collaboration with OFD, Chromaesthesia, and YERB, the project reimagined the club space as a responsive visual system, where sound directly generated light and form. Through real-time audio processing and projection mapping, the performance extended the DJ set into an immersive visual architecture, amplifying the rhythm of the night and intensifying the shared experience on the dance floor.

In collaboration with the music label OFD, live audio was captured on-site and visuals were generated in real time. TouchDesigner and Ableton Live were used for real-time audio-visual interaction, combined with projection mapping for the live visual presentation.
