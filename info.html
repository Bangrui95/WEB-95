Practice Areas ...

My research focuses on visual communication systems, spatial installations, and interactive media. Within these areas, computational prototyping, algorithmic thinking, and data-driven strategies shape immersive environments and structured visual outcomes.

With a foundation in graphic design, my work has evolved toward interaction design, integrating hardware development and front-end technologies. Using JavaScript, Python, TouchDesigner, and Arduino, I create interactive systems and real-time multimedia experiences that connect code, data, and physical space.

In addition, I work with machine learning frameworks, independently building AI models and applying them within creative contexts. I have developed a StyleGAN-based generative system and a localised personalised information processing system, exploring how intelligent systems and data structures influence interaction, clarity, and user experience across digital and physical environments.
